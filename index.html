About Me

I'm a self-taught developer.
I first fell in love with computers in sixth grade when my dad brought home a PC running a Pentium II processor and I started to play PC games. During my high school days, I learned Photoshop 6.0 and HTML. The movie titled “So Close” inspired me to take Computer Science in college.
During my last semester in college, our thesis professor suggested we work on an Android project. My thesis partner and I decided to develop an Android role-playing game based on our favorite anime, Detective Conan but the player will be investigating the main character's father's disappearance bumping into a murder case to a secret Mafia organization where the character's father was also involved.
After graduating college, I started my career in Android development, then I learned web development later on.

When not at work, I hone my skills in playing musical instruments and enjoys listening to classical and jazz music; I dream to own a music studio.
